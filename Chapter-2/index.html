Hello! This is the Home Page of Web Development Assessment 1 Chapter 2! This chapter will focus on Pokémon Platinum's Plot, Key Information, Creative Team, Characters, and Reviews for the game.

Here is a quick overview of the game though: Pokémon Platinum Version is a 2008 role-playing video game developed by Game Freak and published by The Pokémon Company and Nintendo for the Nintendo DS. It is the third version after Pokémon Diamond and Pearl and is part of the fourth generation of the Pokémon video game series. It was released in Japan on September 13, 2008, and later in North America, Australia, and Europe in 2009.
