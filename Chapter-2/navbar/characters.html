Characters

Pokémon Platinum has a lot of fun and unique characters from the random trainers you fight while traveling, to the toughest battlers the player has faced, heres a few:

  • The PLayer Character(Lucas/Dawn by default)
  • The Rival(Barry by default)
  • Prof. Rowan
  • Prof. Rowan's Assistant(Lucas/Dawn, the opposite of the player character)
  • Looker
  • Cyrus
  • The 8 Gym Leaders: Roark, Gardenia, Fantina, Maylene, Crasher Wake, Byron, Candice, Volkner
  • The Elite Four: Bugsy, Bertha, Flint, Lucian
  • The Champion, Cynthia
