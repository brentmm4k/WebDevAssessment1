Key Information

Pokémon Platinum adds in a plethora of new features compared to its predecessors Pokémon Diamond and Pearls such as UI changes, quality of life updates, progression updates and more! Some changes to the story include the order in which you challenge the gym leaders and the inclusion of Giratina into the story, as in Pokémon Diamond and Pearl, the legendary Pokémon Cyrus summons in the climax are the respective Cover Art Pokémon of time and space.

While this feature was added during Diamond and Pearl, the Pokétch(short for Pokémon watch) has a plethora of uses with 25 different apps(e.g. digital watch, calculator, memo pad, a Pokémon list, and many more!).

Pokémon Platinum also introduces the GTS(Global Trading System) that lets players globally trade their Pokémon anonymously using Wi-fi. The most significant piece information is the platform in which Pokémon Platinum is being played in, The Nintendo DS is a foldable handheld game console produced by Nintendo, released globally across 2004 and 2005. The DS, an initialism for "Developers' System" or "Dual Screen", introduced distinctive new features to handheld games. With the DS being Dual Screen, the aforementioned Pokémon watch can be seen in the bottom screen while the overworld gameplay is displayed on the top screen.
