Plot

The game begins with the player selecting their gender and character name and being thrusted into the world of Pokémon Platinum. After leaving your room you meet with your rival(who can be named before the game starts as well) and as soon as you leave your town you meet with Prof. Rowan, and after a brief discussion he decides to entrust you and your rival with completing the Pokédex, obtaining all eight gym badges, and defeating the elite four and champion. Along your journey you will meet an evil team named Team Galactic, their goal is unknown for now but a detective known as "Looker" warns you they are up to no good. While collecting the gym badges you will encounter, battle, and catch Pokémon along the way, as well as tussle with Team Galactic from time to time.

Before you take on your 8th and final gym badge you finally figure out Team Galactic's plan to wipe the universe using the legendary Pokémon of time and space "Dialga" and "Palkia", however, Team Galactic's plans grow awry and instead releases the legendary Pokémon of antimatter "Giratina" who abducts the leader of Team Galactic "Cyrus". Along with "Cynthia" the current Pokémon champion who you met earlier in the game, you take down Cyrus once and for all and capture Giratina. After saving the world, the player obtains their 8th gym badge and makes their way to Victory Road, and after traversing Victory Road the player is tasked with defeating the Elite Four and the reigning champion Cynthia in a battle to be crowned the new Pokémon champion.
